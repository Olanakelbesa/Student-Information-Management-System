Who are we?


Since 2001,Gura has been inspiring students to lead a purposeful life, to thrive, and to make a positive impact on the world of today and tomorrow. in this two decades, we create an environment suitable for students to interact with the real world in their class. With the help of senior teachers we empower young students to accomplish their goal.come and join our community.

Why Ggura ?


Gura is known for providing best education for students from 9th to 12th grade. We use student based learning system to make our students creative and help them to develop their skills. Long time experience of teaching make our teachers special.

Our mission is to develop intellectual,ethical,creativity foundation on our students to create community for better world.
